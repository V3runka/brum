How to care

1. Reapply

Apply lip balm whenever it you need. Use it several times a day and a thicker layer for the night.

2. Care

Your lips need the same care as the rest of your face. Make sure to thoroughly clean any lipstick or gloss before you go to bed.

3. Protect

The skin of your lips is very sensitive. Use the Labello Sun Protection Lip Balm in snowy mountains or at sunny beaches.

4. Hydrate

Lips need hydration too! And not just in the form of a moisturizing balm. Do not forget to drink enough water during the day.
